CALL FOR PAPERS
* WORKSHOP
  The 9th International Workshop on Symbolic and Numerical Reasoning about games (SNR) aims to provide a platform for exploring symbolic and numerical techniques for reasoning about games, with applications in verification, synthesis, reactive systems, probabilistic models, and related areas.
* SUBMISSIONS
  SNR solicits submissions for contributed talks in the form of extended abstracts, written in LIPIcs style and of up to 2 pages excluding references. Submissions describing ongoing work, new results, and work published elsewhere are welcome. All submissions will undergo a lightweight peer-reviewing process. Authors of selected submissions presenting original unpublished work will subsequently be invited to submit a full version of their work for publication in formal proceedings, to be published by EPTCS. Submissions will be judged on their expected interest and relevance to the theme of the workshop.
  Submissions can be made via EasyChair: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=snr26
* TOPICS
   Numerical and symbolic methods in games, Automata theory for strategy synthesis, Verification using games, Logical methods for games, Quantitative aspects of games, Strategy synthesis in probabilistic models, Heuristics for solving games
