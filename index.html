SIGLOG Monthly / LICS Newsletters

SIGLOG Monthly is an electronic newsletter sent out monthly to the LICS mailing list. It contains brief summaries of conference announcements, new book announcements, job announcements and other announcements of interest to the logic in computer science community.

Please submit your announcements to the newsletter following the submission instructions. Usually, the newsletter is sent on the first day of each month, so please time your submissions accordingly.

To (un)subcribe to the mailing list, send an email with "(un)subscribe" in the message body to las-lics-request@lists.tu-berlin.de.
If you require help with using the mailing list, send an email with the keyword "help" in the message body to las-lics-request@lists.tu-berlin.de.
